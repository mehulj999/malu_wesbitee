OVERVIEW

Malu Platinum City

Gulbarga's Malu Platinum City plots is a new residential planned development project.Malu Platinum City is a residential enclave that showcases the best of Malu Group's luxury living segment. The project offers vast premium villa plots with exquisite features in the heart of the city.The builder promises to provide a high-quality living experience to the Gulbarga neighborhood, with magnificent architecture and a comparable lifestyle in the heart of the city.It has great connectivity and is centrally positioned in Gulbarga, close to current and planned communities and infrastructure.
A fully equipped clubhouse, landscaped gardens, gymnasium, swimming pool, recreation rooms, outdoor sports courts, children's play area, party hall, and precisely planned with utmost regard to state-of-the-art 24/7 security are just a few of the amenities of Malu Platinum City.
